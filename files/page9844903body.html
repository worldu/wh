Basic requirements for quality antibacterial wipes

The composition of the wipes should remain "functional" for a long time, without losing its properties during storage. Sticky napkins are not allowed, stains must not remain on the clean surface of furniture and stains must not remain on clothes. The napkin should smell good or not. Alcohol wipes are more likely to target bacteria, but it is best not to buy them, because they are not very useful for the skin and often cause irritation.
